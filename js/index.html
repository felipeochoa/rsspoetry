Read classic poetry in your RSS feed.

How does it work?

Select a collection, start date, and publish time in the form below. Then paste the generated link into your RSS reader to subscribe.

New poems will be published to the generated feed every day at the specified time until the end of the collection.

Can I add my own collection?

Yes! If there’s a public domain poetry collection you'd like to add, you can create an issue here, including a link to the collection
